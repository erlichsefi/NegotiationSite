Let's Negotiate.

You are negotiating with your boss about the location of the next company trip.
Each one of you holds an ordinal preference order.
We will present you with both when the game will start.

How to negotiate?

In the beginning, one of you will be in the offering position and the other will respond.
Then you will switch positions.
If you are offering, select an location from all locations present, and Click "Send"
If you are responding, you will be present with the offer made to you, then you can either Accept or Reject it

What is the catch?

You can't offer an outcome that was already rejected by either one.
If there only one outcome you MUST accept it.
