Join in:

Please write a content email.
Then you can select if to play vs PC or play vs other player.
Notice that "Play as p1" or "Play as p1" can be disabled if the player already joined in.

Responding Screen:

This is the screen you will see when you need to respond to an offer, here "C" was offered to you.
You can see if you are playing as p1 or p2, and your preferences as well as the other player preferences.
Then you need to click "X" if you want to reject the offer "C", or "Y" if you want to accept offer "C".

Offering Screen:

This is the screen you will see when you need to offer an outcome.
Again, You can see if you are playing as p1 or p2, and your preferences as well as the other player preferences.
But Now you can see all the outcomes available
Then you need to click on the outcome you want to offer, and "SEND OFFER".
