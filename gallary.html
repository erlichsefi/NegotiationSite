Join:

Please write a content email.
Then you can select if to play as PvsP or PvsPC
Notice that "Play as p1" or "Play as p1" can be disabled if a player joined in.

Responding Screen:

That is the screen you will see as the when you will need to respond to an offers.
You can see if you are playing as p1 or p2, and you preferences as well as the other player preferences.
You then need to click "X" if you want to reject offer "C" or "Y" if you want to accept offer "C".

Offering Screen:

That is the screen you will see as the when you will need to offer an outcome.
Again, You can see if you are playing as p1 or p2, and you preferences as well as the other player preferences.
But Now you can see all the outcomes available
You then need to click on the outcome you want to offer, and then "SEND OFFER".
